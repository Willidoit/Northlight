Privacy Policy — Northlight

Last updated: 2026

Northlight is a local-first macOS application. We do not upload your photos, edits, or catalogs to our servers. Any network requests made by the app are described here:

- Crash reporting (optional, opt-in) — anonymous and limited.
- License validation on purchase — minimal information needed to verify a license.

We do not use user photos to train machine learning models and we do not sell personal data.

For questions, contact us at northlight.app@gmail.com
